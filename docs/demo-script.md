# TnG FinHack 26 — Demo Script

**Total Duration:** 3 minutes 30 seconds  
**Presenter Notes:** Speak with energy. Pause for visual impact. Do not rush the "magic moment."

---

## 0:00–0:30 — DASHBOARD OVERVIEW

**Actions:**
1. Open the app at `http://localhost:5173` (Dashboard loads by default).
2. Stand back. Let the screen breathe for 2–3 seconds.

**What to say:**
> "Good morning judges. This is the **TnG Credit Intelligence Platform** — real-time credit assessment built for Malaysian SMEs."

**Point out (left to right across KPI cards):**
- **Total Credits Issued** — RM 4.2M with a +12.5% trend
- **Average Risk Score** — 342, trending down (good!)
- **Active IoT Devices** — 1,247 devices online across merchant locations
- **Reconciliation Status** — 98.7% auto-matched

**Action:** Scroll down slightly to reveal the Recent Transactions table.

> "Every transaction you see here has been scored by our ML model in under half a second."

---

## 0:30–1:00 — IoT MONITOR

**Actions:**
1. Click **IoT Monitor** in the left sidebar.
2. Let the live telemetry panel load.

**What to say:**
> "We have IoT devices deployed at merchant locations collecting telemetry — GPS, battery health, signal strength, CPU temperature."

**Point out:**
- Live GPS coordinates drifting around Kuala Lumpur
- Battery levels (e.g., 94.2%)
- Signal strength bars (-40 to -80 dBm)
- Document metadata hashes flowing in real time

> "This telemetry flows through the **Alibaba Cloud IoT Platform** right here in Malaysia."

**Key line (pause for emphasis):**
> "Only **non-financial device data** lives on Alibaba — all financial data stays on AWS Singapore. That boundary is enforced at the architecture level, not just in policy."

---

## 1:00–1:15 — THE MAGIC MOMENT

**Actions:**
1. Click **Wallet Credit** in the sidebar.
2. Take a breath. Build anticipation.
3. Say the line.
4. **Press the trigger button.**
5. Step back. Let the animation happen.

**What to say:**
> "Now watch this — one press, instant credit assessment."

**Visual payoff:**
- The RM counter animates rapidly up to **RM 212,500**
- Confetti fires across the screen
- Risk gauge settles on a score
- Feature importance bars appear

**Point to the latency badge:**
> "Processed in **under 500 milliseconds** using our gradient boosting model."

**Point to the risk gauge and feature breakdown:**
> "This isn't a black box. We show exactly why the decision was made."

---

## 1:15–1:45 — RISK ANALYTICS (ML EXPLAINABILITY)

**Actions:**
1. Click **Risk Analytics** in the sidebar.
2. Gesture to the full-screen charts.

**What to say:**
> "This isn't a hardcoded score — it's a real gradient boosting model trained on 2,000 synthetic SME records that mirror Malaysian market conditions."

**Point to the feature importance chart:**
> "Payment consistency and business tenure are the top factors. The model learned that from the data, not from us telling it what to do."

**Point to the score distribution histogram:**
> "You can see the full distribution of risk scores across our portfolio. Every decision is traceable."

**Key line:**
> "**Every decision is explainable and auditable.** That's not a feature — it's a compliance requirement."

---

## 1:45–2:15 — ARCHITECTURE & DATA SOVEREIGNTY

**Actions:**
1. Click **IoT Monitor** or **Dashboard** — use the live app to tell the architecture story visually.
2. If an Architecture Diagram page is available, navigate there. Otherwise, use the live data flows as your visual aid.

**What to say:**
> "Let me show you why multi-cloud isn't just a buzzword for us."

**Point out color-coded zones (on screen or in diagram):**
- **Blue zone** = AWS Singapore — all financial compute, credit decisions, transaction storage
- **Orange zone** = Alibaba Cloud Malaysia — IoT ingestion, device management, telemetry

**Highlight the RED X markers (if visible on diagram):**
> "See these red X's? **Financial data NEVER crosses into Alibaba Cloud.** Wallet balances, KYC data, risk scores — they never leave AWS."

**Key line:**
> "**Multi-cloud by design — data sovereignty is built in, not bolted on.**"

---

## 2:15–2:35 — RECONCILIATION

**Actions:**
1. Click **Reconciliation** in the sidebar.
2. Click the **"Download Excel"** button.
3. Open the downloaded file.

**What to say:**
> "This is what our operations team sees every morning."

**Show the Excel file:**
> "Real reconciliation report — four sheets, compliance audit trail included. Every transaction, every risk score, every compliance check."

**Key line:**
> "This isn't a mockup. This is the actual report generated by our Lambda function and delivered straight to the ops team."

---

## 2:35–3:05 — COMPLIANCE HUB

**Actions:**
1. Click **Compliance Hub** in the sidebar.
2. Walk through the flow diagram slowly.

**What to say:**
> "Let's talk compliance — because in fintech, nothing else matters if you don't get this right."

**Walk through the flow diagram:**
- **TnG** sits at the top as the **data oracle**
- We provide structured risk scores
- The **licensed P2P lending partner** makes the actual lending decision
- The borrower receives funds from the partner, not from TnG

**Point to the separation:**
> "TnG is the data oracle — we provide risk scores. The licensed P2P partner makes the lending decision."

**Key line:**
> "**TnG does not originate, fund, or service any loans.** That makes us fully BNM compliant. We're a technology layer, not a lender."

---

## 3:05–3:30 — WRAP UP + Q&A PREP

**Actions:**
1. Click back to **Dashboard**.
2. Stand centered. Summarize with confidence.

**What to say:**
> "So here's what we built: **Real ML.** Real multi-cloud. Real compliance. From IoT telemetry at a merchant storefront in KL to a credit decision in under three seconds."

> "The model is trained. The infrastructure is deployed. The compliance framework is documented. We're ready to pilot with real merchants."

**Pause. Smile.**

> "Thank you. We'd love to take your questions."
